## Ingredients

- Sourdough bread
- Avocado
- Feta
- Lemon juice
- Chili oil

## Steps

1. Toast bread until crisp.
2. Mash avocado with lemon.
3. Spread, then top with feta.
4. Finish with a little chili oil.

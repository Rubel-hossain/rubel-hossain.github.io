## Ingredients

- Baby potatoes
- Olive oil
- Garlic
- Rosemary
- Sea salt

## Steps

1. Boil potatoes for 8 minutes.
2. Smash lightly and drizzle olive oil.
3. Roast at high heat until crisp.
4. Toss with garlic and rosemary.

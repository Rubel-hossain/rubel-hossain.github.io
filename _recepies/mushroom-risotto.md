## Ingredients

- Arborio rice
- Mushrooms
- Onion
- Vegetable stock
- Parmesan

## Steps

1. Sauté onion and mushrooms.
2. Stir in rice and toast briefly.
3. Add stock gradually while stirring.
4. Finish with parmesan and black pepper.

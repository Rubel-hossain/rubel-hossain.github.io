## Ingredients

- Brown rice
- Cucumber
- Carrot
- Chickpeas
- Tahini sauce

## Steps

1. Cook rice and cool slightly.
2. Slice vegetables thinly.
3. Add warm chickpeas.
4. Drizzle tahini sauce and serve.

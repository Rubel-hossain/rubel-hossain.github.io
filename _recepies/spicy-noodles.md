## Ingredients

- Noodles
- Garlic
- Chili flakes
- Soy sauce
- Sesame oil

## Steps

1. Cook noodles and reserve a little water.
2. Sizzle garlic and chili in oil.
3. Toss noodles with soy and sesame.
4. Adjust texture with reserved water.
